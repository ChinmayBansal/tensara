## Problem Statement
Implement a CUDA kernel to perform element-wise addition of two vectors:
$c_i = a_i + b_i$

## Input
- Vectors $a$ and $b$ of length $N$

## Output
- Vector $c$ of length $N$ containing the element-wise sum
